## 4s Coed Volleyball Tournament Rules

### 1. Team Composition
- Each team consists of 4 players on the court at all times.
- Teams are mixed-gender and must have at least one male and one female on the court at all times.

### 2. Scoring
- Matches are played to 21 points.
- Win by 2, cap at 25 (first team to 25 wins even if the opposing team has 24).

### 3. Scoring (championship game)
- One match to 21 pts. Win by 2, no cap.
- Set 2 (if losers-bracket team won Set 1): one match to 21 pts. Win by 2, cap at 25.

### 4. Serving
- Serve must be made behind the end line.
- Servers get one fault or redo if they commit a line violation (e.g., stepping over the end line) during the serve. A second fault results in a point for the opposing team. Servers get one redo per rotation at the position, not one per rally.
- Net serves are in play if they go over and land in bounds.
- Blocking or attacking a serve is not permitted and will result in the opposing team's point. (Attacking includes jump sets and swings on or off the ground.)

### 5. Gameplay
- Each team gets up to 3 touches to return the ball over the net (bump, set, spike).
- No player may hit the ball twice in a row (except on a block, which doesn't count as a touch).
- The ball must stay in bounds; lines are considered in.
- No carrying, lifting, or throwing the ball — clean hits only.
- No open-handed tips over the net (single, open-handed pushes/tips/sets are NOT allowed even if the ball is perfectly square with shoulders. All single-handed touches over the net must be closed-fisted or a swing with a rigid hand).
- All sets or double open-handed overs must be perfectly square with shoulders.
- If an agreement cannot be made on a call, the point is replayed.
- Double sets and open-hand returns are allowed if the ball is a pass to a player on your side of the net. Anything over the net must be a clean set square with your shoulders, but you are allowed to double a set if it's a pass to a teammate.

### 6. Rotations & Substitutions
- Players rotate clockwise each time the team regains the serve.
- Servers must also rotate and keep original order throughout the entire set.
- The server is technically the only back-row player, therefore not permitted to attack or block at the net. Any attacks made cannot be while jumping.

### 7. Net Rules / Blocking
- Players may not touch the net during play.
- Crossing the centerline under the net is allowed, as long as it doesn't interfere with the opposing team.
- Any interference or contact under the net ends the rally and will result in the opposing team's point.
- Blocking while the ball is still fully on the opponent's side is not permitted, and will be called for interference. The only exception is if the 3rd touch has already been completed and the opponent has finished touching the ball.

### 8. Timeouts
- Each team gets one 30-second timeout per set.
- No carryover of unused timeouts between sets.
